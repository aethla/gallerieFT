Meet the face behind the art!

Thea

Althea, "Thea" our portrait drawer! She's the one that introduced Fako to digital drawing for the first time. She studies AI, but when she's free Thea likes to unwind by reading short stories, trying out piano, watching movies, and exploring all the different ways that art presents itself in our lives

Fako

Fakeha, "Fako" she draws the landscapes! Fakeha started art recently and found drawing skies cathartic. She studies AI with Thea, but in her free time she spends it baking, book writing or (obviously) creating art. She also enjoys editing videos and creating audios. One of her aspirations is to complete a portrait without procastinating, but so far she has yet to achieve her 'dream' :).
